## 3. App Store Description (English)
*À utiliser pour la fiche produit principale (Long description).*

**Short Description / Subtitle:**
Scan, summarize, and analyze your documents instantly with AI.

**Long Description:**
Transform your phone into a powerful AI assistant with **documate**. Whether it’s a complex legal contract, a long business report, or everyday administrative paperwork, documate helps you understand and manage your information in seconds.

**Key Features:**
- **Smart Scanning**: Use your camera or upload files (PDF, images, text) to digitize any document instantly.
- **AI-Powered Analysis**: Stop reading through hundreds of pages. Get clear summaries and detailed insights powered by advanced AI models.
- **Smart Templates**: Automatically generate drafts, emails, or professional summaries based on your analyzed documents.
- **Personal History**: Keep an organized record of all your scans and find the information you need, whenever you need it.
- **Global Context**: Multi-language support and region-specific options for accurate local context.

**How it works:**
1. **Scan**: Take a photo or upload a file.
2. **Settings**: Select your region or add specific questions for the AI.
3. **Analyze**: Receive your summary and insights immediately.

**Experience documate Pro:**
Unlock unlimited scans, remove ads, and access our full suite of premium templates. Free users can also earn extra scans easily by watching short reward videos.

Simplify your paperwork and save hours of work with **documate**.
